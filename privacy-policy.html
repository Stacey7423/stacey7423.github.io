Cookies

We use cookies on our website. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences, track website usage, and personalize your experience. We use both first-party cookies (set by us) and third-party cookies (set by other websites). The main purpose of cookies is to improve our website and enhance the user experience. We may use third-party analytics services, such as Google Analytics, which also use cookies.

  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your language preference) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, please note that if you block or delete cookies, some features of our website may not function properly. You can find more information about how to manage cookies in your browser’s help documentation.
